The business focuses on simple, functional 3D-printed products designed to solve everyday problems. Products are sold through online marketplaces including Etsy, eBay, and Amazon. Rather than producing decorative items, I focused on utility-driven designs.

## Engineering & Execution

<ProjectImage src="/wyzehub.png" scale={40} alt="Product Example" />

I researched on platforms such as Etsy and Amazon to find specific products that were missing from the marketplace. I designed functional products that were easy to 3D print and were genuinely useful to people. An example of a product I sell is a wall mount for a smart home product that many people use. Many accessories for this brand and product exist, but a wall mount was missing which I capitalized on.

## Results

The business generated $8,000+ in sales across Etsy, eBay, and Amazon, with 800+ orders fulfilled. Through this process, I learned how to make functional, cost-effective products that sell well. I also learned how to market products and ensure positive feedback.
