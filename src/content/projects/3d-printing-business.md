## Business Operations

Running the 3D printing business required managing multiple aspects simultaneously, from client communication and design consultation to production scheduling and quality assurance. I handled all aspects of the operation, from initial customer inquiries to final product delivery.

## Key Services

- Custom part design and prototyping
- Small-batch production runs
- Design consultation and optimization
- Post-processing and finishing services
- Rapid turnaround for urgent projects

## Technical Skills Developed

- **3D Printing Technologies:** Mastered FDM printing with various materials (PLA, PETG, TPU)
- **Design Optimization:** Learned to optimize designs for 3D printing, including support structures and material selection
- **Quality Control:** Developed systematic approaches to ensure consistent print quality
- **Production Management:** Balanced multiple orders while maintaining quality standards
- **Customer Relations:** Managed client expectations and provided regular updates on project progress

## Business Impact

The business provided valuable experience in entrepreneurship and manufacturing. I learned to balance technical quality with business efficiency, manage customer relationships, and deliver products that met client specifications and deadlines.

## Lessons Learned

- Time management is critical when balancing production with other commitments
- Clear communication with clients prevents misunderstandings and ensures satisfaction
- Quality control processes are essential for maintaining reputation
- Material selection and print settings significantly impact final product quality
- Customer feedback is invaluable for improving services and processes
