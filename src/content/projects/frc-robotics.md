FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an international high school robotics competition where teams design, build, and program robots to compete in challenging game scenarios.

## Team Leadership

I served as the mechanical subteam lead, coordinating design efforts, managing fabrication timelines, and ensuring all mechanical systems integrated seamlessly with electrical and software components.

## Key Contributions

- **Drivetrain Design:** Designed and optimized a 6-wheel swerve drive system for maximum maneuverability and speed
- **Manipulator Mechanisms:** Created custom gripper and lift systems for game piece manipulation
- **CAD Modeling:** Used SolidWorks to model all components and generate manufacturing drawings
- **Fabrication:** Operated CNC machines, 3D printers, and manual tools to build robot components
- **Integration:** Worked closely with electrical and software teams to ensure seamless system integration

## Competition Results

Our team competed in regional competitions, demonstrating strong performance in both autonomous and teleoperated periods. The robot successfully completed all required game tasks and advanced to elimination rounds.

## Technologies Used

- SolidWorks CAD Software
- CNC Machining
- 3D Printing (FDM)
- Metal Fabrication
- Team Project Management
- Strategic Game Analysis
