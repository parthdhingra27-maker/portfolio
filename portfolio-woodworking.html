Custom Desk

  • Category: Design & Fabrication
  • Date: 2025

Overview

I designed and built a custom desk for my 3D printer. The project involved creating a simple CAD model of the design, cutting the wood using power tools such as a miter saw and circular saw, and applying a stain and clear coat for durability. Through this project, I learned how to translate a CAD concept into a functional piece of furniture, improved my woodworking skills, and created a stable workstation that reduces vibration during prints.

Instrument Stand

  • Category: Design & Fabrication
  • Date: 2025

Overview

I collaborated with a friend on his Eagle Scout project to design and build violin stands for a local elementary school. My contributions included helping create the design, measuring, cutting, and assembling the wooden components while ensuring stability and durability for daily use. This project combined woodworking with community service and taught me how to design and fabricate a useful project.
