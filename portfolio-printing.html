3D Printing Business

  • Category: Entrepreneurship & Engineering
  • Years: 2023 – Present

Overview

I created and operate an online 3D printing business that designs and sells niche functional products such as wall mounts and other household accessories. I conduct product research to identify unmet needs in the market, use CAD to design solutions, and prototype them with 3D printers. To date, I have completed over 600+ orders and sell across multiple platforms, including Amazon, Etsy, and eBay. Through this venture, I’ve gained hands-on experience combining technical design with entrepreneurship to build a successful online business.
